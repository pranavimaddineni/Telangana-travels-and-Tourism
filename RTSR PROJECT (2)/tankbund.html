Buddha statue

The most prominent site of Hussain Sagar Lake is the beautiful Buddha Statue perched on top of a rocky Gibraltar Rock. With a height of over 16 metre, this gigantic sculpture offers a phenomenal view and attracts swarms of tourists.

Lumbini Park

Located on the sides of the Hussain Sagar Lake, Lumbini Park is a well-landscaped green urban corner in a city surrounded by bustling activity. This park has stunning rock features, Japanese gardens and many interesting recreational facilities that attract families and nature lovers.

NTR Gardens

NTR Gardens, known as Necklace Road Park, is a green open space close to the lake. It is a big green oasis with lush vegetation where people walk and admire a gorgeous and unique musical fountain show with coordinated water displays and different lighting.

Snow World

Snow World in Hyderabad is an indoor amusement park featuring snow play areas, ice skating, skiing, snow sculptures, and snowfall experiences, offering visitors a unique winter wonderland. Located near Hussain Sagar Lake, it provides warm clothing, food stalls, and lockers, ensuring a fun and comfortable visit for all ages.

Birla Mandir

Birla Mandir in Hyderabad, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, is a stunning marble temple showcasing Dravidian, Rajasthani, and Utkala architecture. Located on Naubath Pahad, it features intricate carvings, additional shrines, serene gardens, and offers a panoramic city view. Open daily with free entry.

Necklace Road

Necklace Road in Hyderabad is a scenic boulevard encircling Hussain Sagar Lake, connecting NTR Gardens and Sanjeevaiah Park. It offers parks, eateries, and boating, providing a popular recreational spot with beautiful lake views and vibrant nightlife.

B.M. Birla Planetarium

B.M. Birla Planetarium in Hyderabad, located near Birla Mandir, offers immersive astronomical shows and exhibits on space science and technology. It's a popular educational and recreational destination for visitors interested in learning about the universe.

Jalavihar Water Park

Jalavihar Water Park in Hyderabad, located near Hussain Sagar Lake, offers exciting water slides, wave pools, and family-friendly attractions. It's a popular spot for fun and relaxation, catering to visitors of all ages with its diverse water-based activities.
