Spellbinding Studio Tour

It’s a day-long engagement with the make-believe world! The specially designed studio tour will take you around the magical realm, explore the cinematic sets and locations, thematic attractions, live shows, rides, children’s attractions, eco-tour, food and shopping.

Bahubali Set

As you step into the blockbuster Bahubali sets, immerse in the grandeur of Mahishmati kingdom. The grand courtyard, the palace compound, majestic throne, the giant statue of Bhallala Dev, towering pillars, and weaponry etc. create vivid memories of the epic saga

Bird Park

A haven for bird lovers! The Bird Park houses a diverse range of exotic winged beauties in all hues and stripes. Enjoy the vibrant colours and melodious chirping as you stroll through this serene oasis.

Kids’ Amusement Rides

Ramoji Film City is a paradise for kids with a variety of thrilling rides and attractions. From merry-go-rounds and mini roller coasters to bumping cars, there's something fun for kids of all ages.

Filmi Duniya

Travel the world in just a few steps at Filmi Duniya. Take a journey into the world of fairy-tale and be thrilled by the wonders of the world, Rio Carnival and the iconic Eiffel Tower – all at once.

Fundustan

A magical zone for children! Here, the little ones are presented with a bonanza of thrills, fun rides and games, ensuring endless excitement and joy. The spine-chilling experience at Borasura – the themed walk-through adds an extra layer of excitement.

Kripalu Caves

Explore the mystical Kripalu Caves! Adorned with exquisite rock formations and intricate carvings, the intriguing passages inspire beauty and tranquillity. The serene and contemplative ambience is perfect for a peaceful retreat!

Live Entertainment Shows

Enjoy a variety of live shows featuring talented performers, from traditional dance and musical acts to performances in blacklight costumes and action-packed stunt show, providing non-stop entertainment.

Delightful Carnival

Join in the joyous fun and festivity at the delightful Carnival. The vividly decorated tableaus hit the dream avenues with fairies in feathers, clowns, stilt walkers and jugglers making a stunning spectacle!
